NextStep.io is an online personal wellness coaching service with programs that make use of popular activity trackers. I joined the team in late 2013 as a designer and front-end developer. I have filled those roles as well as a variety of others required in an early stage startup.

While with NextStep.io, I have lead the design and evolution of the company's brand, homepage, web application, mobile application, brand illustrations, email templates, user manuals, and assorted print materials (including posters, apparel, stickers).
